Apply to Become a XPNET Ambassador

Out-of-the-box multichain support for minted NFTs: connect to XP.network bridge to move NFTs and funds seamlessly across 10+ blockchain.

Ambassadors, preferably local people, are expected to create local communities on behalf of the platform and introduce its products and services to potential users. They can also collect feedbacks from users.

The XP.network Ambassador Program will increase XPNET brand awareness and influence and also help to bring global cryptocurrency enthusiasts together.
