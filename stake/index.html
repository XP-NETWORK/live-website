FAQ

XPNET is the native token of XP.network. Its main use is to reward the validators for confirming bridge transactions. It’s also used for staking and to encourage the development of the ecosystem through partnerships, collaborations with advisors, etc. XPNET runs on Binance Smart Chain, which ensures high transaction processing speed and low fees.

XPNET was initially released to the public during the three parallel IDO and IEO events on BSCPad, Occam.Razer, and Gate.io. The total supply is XXX XPNET.

The smart contract for XPNET is 0x8cf8238abf7b933Bf8BB5Ea2C7E4Be101c11de2A

It is a blockchain transaction of locking the ERC-20/BEP-20 tokens in order to earn rewards. In other words, we put your tokens in a safety — deposit box and award you every second with a fraction of XPNET. The size of the fraction depends on the period of staking.

When you stake XPNET, you receive rewards that come in two forms:
1) More XPNET tokens at the end of the staking period. The APY varies from 45% to 125%, respectively.
2) A unique chain-agnostic NFT. It allows its holder to withdraw the XPNET rewards, but it also has an artistic value of its own and can be sold independently.

XP.network is the first blockchain protocol to reward stakers with NFTs in addition to the regular tokens. When you stake XPNET, you’ll be able to mint a unique chain-agnostic NFT, which acts as an access key to the rewards.
Whoever holds the NFT can claim the rewards at the end of the staking period.

You can sell the chain-agnostic NFT at any moment in the secondary market (in a marketplace or directly), but you will lose the right to claim the staking rewards. For this reason, it’s not recommended to sell the NFT until the end of the staking period. You will still be able to withdraw the original XPNET staking deposit, of course, even if you sell the NFT.

Please use the calculator above to calculate the reward estimate. Keep in mind that token prices are volatile, so there is no way to know the USD value of your rewards in advance. The USD estimate provided by the calculator is based on the current XPNET price and is for information purposes only.
